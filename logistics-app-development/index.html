Frequently Asked Questions

How can you deliver a logistics app so fast?

We use an AI-assisted development workflow: AI automates repetitive coding, test generation, and QA checks, while senior engineers oversee architecture, security, and logistics-specific logic. This cuts delivery time to about one quarter of a traditional agency—4-8 weeks instead of 16-32—without sacrificing quality.

What technology stack do you use for logistics apps?

Our standard stack is React Native for cross-platform mobile, Node.js with PostgreSQL on the backend, and AWS for cloud hosting. We integrate with logistics APIs (Google Maps, Samsara, KeepTruckin) and can add offline support, barcode scanning, and real-time tracking.

How do you ensure security and compliance in a logistics app?

We follow OWASP MASVS for mobile security, encrypt data in transit and at rest, and design for SOC 2 controls where needed. Role-based access, audit logs, and secure API gateways protect sensitive load and driver information.

How much does logistics app development cost in the USA?

Cost depends on features, integrations, and scale. A focused driver app or dispatcher dashboard typically ranges from $50,000 to $150,000, while a full TMS with EDI, fleet management, and analytics can reach $250,000+. We provide a detailed estimate after a discovery session.
